Extraction and creation of elements and compounds

Sulfuric Acid

The process has the following stages.

  1. Sulfur dioxide is first produces
  2. The gases are then purified and heated to 450℃ and passed over a catalyst vanadium oxide (V2O5).
  • An optimum temperature of 450℃ is used and an optimum pressure of atmospheric pressure is used.
  1. Sulfur trioxide is dissolved in concentrated sulfuric acid (98%) to give a substance called oleum.
